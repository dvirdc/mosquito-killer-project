PewPewBug

computer-vision bug spotter · simulator

demo

Tap or click the video to set a manual target; use Clear target to release it (on a computer you can also double-click to clear). To test detection: toggle the Demo bug, or wave a pen tip / toss a small paper ball in view. People and other large moving objects are ignored; a snapshot is captured automatically when bug confidence exceeds 99%. On a Mac, disable Center Stage in Control Center for the widest field of view.

Just for fun. This is a toy simulator built for entertainment and learning. It draws a virtual beam on your screen — it is not a real laser, a pest-control product, or any kind of safety, medical, or scientific device. Don't rely on it for anything that matters, and never point real lasers at people, animals, or eyes.
